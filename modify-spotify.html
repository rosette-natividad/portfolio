Modify Spotify

Problem Solving Through Design

☆ ★ ☆ ★ ☆

Modify Spotify was a group of six members, including myself, for our Problem Solving Through Design project in my Social Informatics course in Fall 2022.
Image from Spotify.
Goal

Our goal was to develop a platform specifically designed for college students, facilitating task management and time organization for optimum efficiency, with the aim of helping students enhance productivity and reduce academic stress.

Problem

People's work efficiency and time management often suffer due to constant distractions. Many college students struggle to effectively manage their workload, often due to limited time or competing priorities in their lives.

Context

College students frequently juggle many courses, assignments, and extracurricular activities in today’s fast-paced academic environment, making efficient time management critical. The rise of digital distractions, like social networking and entertainment, has made it even more difficult to stay focused on assignments. Furthermore, the transition to online learning as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic has blurred the lines between personal and academic life, making it more difficult to keep a disciplined schedule. To address these difficulties, a tool that supports college students in effectively managing their time and avoiding distractions is required to boost their productivity and academic performance.
