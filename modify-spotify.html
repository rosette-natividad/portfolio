Modify Spotify

Problem Solving Through Design was a group project made up of six members in my Social Informatics course in fall 2022. Our team name was Modify Spotify.

Image from Spotify.
Goal

☆ ★ ☆ ★ ☆

Spotify is a popular music streaming platform. Our goal was to improve Spotify’s user experience through design, whether it be remodeling existing features or designing new features. There’s always room for improvement.
